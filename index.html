Beast World exists because there is no program on Earth that takes teenagers and puts them through something this hard, this real, and this relentless... for this long.

This is not study abroad. This is not a service trip. You will live in communities most people will never see, build infrastructure that changes thousands of lives, present your work every week in a language you did not know existed three months ago, and do it all while 400 million people watch.

You compete on a live scoreboard all year. Two hours of Ivy League caliber academics every single day. No exceptions. And every moment is filmed for the world to see.

You walk out as someone who has discovered what they are made of. Not because someone told you, but because you proved it under a year of unrelenting pressure.

Built by Beast Philanthropy × Alpha School

Where You Are Going

Three continents. Real work. Real impact.

Africa

Rotation One. ~10 Weeks

Cocoa villages in rural West Africa. You will build Beast Schools alongside the communities that need them. Solar power, Starlink, wells, classrooms.

"You will know every family by name."

Beast Schools Solar Install Well Construction Starlink Setup

India

Rotation Two. ~10 Weeks

Navigate one of the most complex cultures on Earth. Build projects that serve real needs: clean water, education, economic development. Present your work to community leaders who hold nothing back.

"The hardest place to earn trust. The most rewarding when you do."

Clean Water Education Econ Dev Community Trust

The Amazon

Rotation Three. ~10 Weeks

Remote communities in Ecuador. Extreme conditions. Physical intensity. Everything is harder here. The heat, the terrain, the logistics. Build here and you can build anywhere on Earth.

"If you can build here, you can build anywhere."

Extreme Build Remote Ops Physical Intensity Final Push
