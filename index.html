School Year
Beast World exists because there is no program on Earth that takes teenagers and puts them through something this hard, this real, and this impactful - for this long.

This is not a community service year. It is not volunteer tourism. It is not resume padding.

You will live in communities most people will never see, build infrastructure that changes thousands of lives, present your work every week in a language you didn't know existed three months ago - and do it all while 400 million people watch.

The service is real. The stakes are real. And the transformation is earned.

Built by Beast Philanthropy × Alpha School

Where You're Going

Three continents. Real work. Real impact.

You'll spend 10 weeks in each location with your team of 40. Every rotation, you set an ambitious goal, execute it, and leave something permanent behind.

Africa

Rotation One. ~10 Weeks

Cocoa villages in rural West Africa. You'll build Beast Schools alongside the communities that need them - solar power, Starlink, wells, classrooms. Real construction. Real stakes.

"You'll know every family by name."

Beast Schools Solar Install Well Construction Starlink Setup

India

Rotation Two. ~10 Weeks

Navigate one of the most complex cultures on Earth. Build projects that serve real needs: clean water, education, economic development. Present your work to community leaders who hold nothing back.

"The hardest place to earn trust. The most rewarding when you do."

Clean Water Education Econ Dev Community Trust

The Amazon

Rotation Three. ~10 Weeks

Remote communities in Ecuador. Extreme conditions. Physical intensity. Everything is harder here - the heat, the terrain, the logistics. Build here and you can build anywhere on Earth.

"If you can build here, you can build anywhere."

Extreme Build Remote Ops Physical Intensity Final Push
